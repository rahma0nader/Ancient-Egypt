Name:

The Early Dynastic Period in Egypt is the beginning of the historical era of the country during which the regions of Upper Egypt (south) and Lower Egypt (north) were united as one country under a centralized government. The divine rule of the kings began a recognizable Egyptian culture, including the development of writing, arts, and sciences.
